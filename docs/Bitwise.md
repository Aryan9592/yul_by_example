### Bitwise Operators:
```
    Name        |   Symbol      
________________|___________
    AND         |   &   
    LEFT SHIFT  |   <<
    NOT         |   ~    
    OR          |   |
    RIGHT SHIFT |   >>
    XOR         |   ^           
```
